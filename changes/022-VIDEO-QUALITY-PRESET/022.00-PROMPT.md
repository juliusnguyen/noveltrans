# 022 — Video quality/speed preset

Rendering a full-novel video is slow (~1h render per ~3h audio at 1080p). Give the user a
single "Chất lượng" (quality/speed) setting in the video export box that trades fidelity for
a faster encode, bundling the three levers into one choice:

- resolution (1080p ↔ 720p),
- frame rate (25 ↔ 15 fps),
- the spinning vinyl (on ↔ off — the per-frame `rotate` is the biggest filter cost).

Presets:
- **Cao — 1080p**: 1920×1080, 25fps, spinning vinyl (default, best quality).
- **Nhanh — 720p**: 1280×720, 25fps, spinning vinyl (~3× faster).
- **Nhanh nhất — 720p, không đĩa xoay**: 1280×720, 15fps, static disc (~5× faster).

Persist the choice, and show an estimated render time in the confirm dialog.
